Privacy Policy

moneasy — Simple Expense Tracker

1. Introduction

moneasy ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

Data stored locally on your device (not collected by us):

  • Expense data (amount, category, date, notes, store name)
  • Income data (amount, category, date)
  • Budget and fixed expense settings
  • Asset information
  • Receipt images
  • Category learning rules (store-to-category mappings)

All of this data is stored on your device. We do not have access to this data.

Data we may collect (Automatic Error Reporting):

When a crash or unexpected error occurs, we may automatically collect and transmit the following information to improve the app:

  • Error type and location (code stack trace)
  • App version and OS version
  • Device type (no personally identifiable information is included)

Transmission destination: Google Cloud Functions (infrastructure for app management)
Purpose: Bug fixes and app quality improvement only. Not sold or shared with third parties.

3. AI Processing

The App's Pro features use the following AI processing:

  • Receipt OCR: Receipt images are sent to the Google Gemini Flash API to extract and structure expense information. Images are processed in real-time and are not stored by Google.
  • Voice Input: Text converted from speech is sent to AI APIs to structure expense information. Audio data itself is never sent.
  • AI Monthly Reports & Category Inference: Summary expense data (amounts and category names) is sent to AI APIs to generate analysis.

In all cases, no personally identifiable information (name, contact details, address) is sent.

4. iCloud Sync

The App uses Apple CloudKit to sync data across your iCloud-connected devices. This is an optional feature that can be disabled in your iCloud settings.

If you use the Family Sharing feature, shared data is encrypted and stored in Apple CloudKit's public database. The encryption key is derived from the invitation code and is never stored in CloudKit.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Data necessary for AI processing (Google Gemini Flash API)
  • Error report transmission (Google Cloud Functions)
  • iCloud sync (Apple CloudKit)
  • When required by law

6. Cookies and Advertising

The App does not use cookies. The App does not display advertisements. No third-party tracking SDKs are used.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your data in the following ways:

  • Account Deletion: Go to Settings → "Delete Account" to permanently delete all data (expenses, income, receipts, category settings, etc.).
  • Individual Deletion: Each expense or income entry can be deleted individually.
  • App Deletion: Deleting the app removes all local data from your device.
  • iCloud Data: Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage to delete synced data.

8. Data Security

  • Local data is protected by Apple's device encryption.
  • Sensitive information (authentication tokens) is securely stored in the iOS Keychain.
  • Family sharing data is encrypted using keys derived via HKDF(SHA-256).

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. If significant changes are made, we will notify you within the App.
