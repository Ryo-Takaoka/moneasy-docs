Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Is my data safe?

Yes. All your financial data is stored on your device and the developer never has access to it. iCloud sync is protected by Apple's encryption. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q. Can I transfer my data when switching devices?

If iCloud sync is enabled, simply installing the app on your new device will automatically sync your data.

Q. How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, then select moneasy to cancel. You can continue using Pro features until the end of your current billing period.

Q. How do I delete all my data?

Go to the app's Settings → "Delete Account" to permanently delete all data. This action cannot be undone.

Q. What if receipt scanning accuracy is low?

Take photos in well-lit conditions and make sure the entire receipt fits within the frame. Creases or stains on the receipt may reduce accuracy.

Q. Any tips for tracking expenses effectively?

With moneasy, you can easily record expenses by scanning receipts or using voice input. You don't need to log expenses every day — just batch-scan your receipts. Start by categorizing into broad groups like food and daily necessities.

Q. How does receipt management work?

When you photograph a receipt in moneasy, AI automatically reads and records the amounts, items, and store name. Receipt images are saved within the app, so there's no need to keep paper receipts.

Q. How does voice input work?

With the Pro plan, just say something like "500 yen at the convenience store" and the expense is recorded. AI automatically identifies the amount, category, and store name. The free plan supports input via Siri Shortcuts.

Q. How do I set a budget?

Go to the Budget tab to set monthly budgets. You can also set budgets per category. You'll be notified when you're approaching your budget limit.

Q. Can I share my household budget with family?

With the Pro plan's family sharing feature, you can invite family members using an invitation code to share expense data. Shared data is end-to-end encrypted.

Q. What's the difference between Free and Pro plans?

The free plan includes manual input, Siri input, basic receipt scanning, budget management, and analytics. The Pro plan adds AI voice recording, AI-enhanced receipt scanning, AI advice, family sharing, and CSV import.

Q. Can I import CSV data?

With the Pro plan, you can import CSV data from other budgeting apps or banks. AI automatically maps the CSV columns, so no manual configuration is needed.

Q. Can I manage recurring expenses?

Yes. Register recurring expenses like rent, utilities, and subscriptions, and they will be automatically reflected in your monthly expenses. This is also useful for reviewing your fixed costs.
